Objective

The Goal of "Correct!" is to match all question cards to their respective answer cards, before the timer runs out!

How to play: Steps

Step 1

At the beginning of each round, the player will be presented with a grid of cards, all face-down. Half of these cards have questions on their face, the other half have the answers to those questions.

Step 2

The player must find the matching cards by selecting two cards at a time to flip over. If the cards are a match, they remain face-up. If not, the cards are flipped back over, and the player must remember their location.

Step 3

If the player succeeds in matching all their cards, the player wins the game. The player has successfully learned more definitions through this fun, engaging game. Great Job!
